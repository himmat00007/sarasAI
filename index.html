Learn directly from AI professionals

Along with renowned professors worldwide, we have leading AI professionals to teach students. They learn from the people shaping the industry, giving them up-to-date knowledge and skills.

Saras AI will operate under a global model, giving our students the opportunity to network and collaborate with peers from around the world. This will broaden their horizons and expose them to different perspectives and approaches

Holistic

Development

We go beyond just teaching technical skills. Saras AI will also focus on developing our students' life skills such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving. This will prepare them not only for their careers but also for life in general.

Accelerated Job-readiness

With an option to enrol into our 2-years Associate degree program, we provide an accelerated path to our students for being job-ready in the field of AI. The curriculum is designed to equip them with all needed skills, faster.
